About Renta

Built to make renting a car feel simple

We started Renta because booking a rental car shouldn't mean phone tag, surprise fees or fine print. Just clear pricing, real availability, and a set of keys waiting for you.

Our story

From one lot in Kathmandu to 40+ cities

Renta launched as a small booking desk with a handful of sedans. Renters kept telling us the same thing: pricing at other companies was confusing and pickup was slow. So we rebuilt the process around two ideas — show the full price up front, and let people book in minutes from their phone.

Today our fleet spans economy hatchbacks to executive sedans and passenger vans, partnered with local operators so cars are always nearby, not across town.
